Pasta Recipes

Pasta is a staple food made from unleavened dough of wheat flour and water, sometimes with eggs. The dough is shaped into various forms and then boiled in water. To make pasta at home: Make the dough by mixing flour, eggs (optional), and water. Knead until smooth and elastic. Roll the dough out very thin using a rolling pin or pasta machine. Cut the rolled dough into your desired pasta shapes, like spaghetti, penne, or ravioli. Cook the fresh pasta in boiling salted water for 2-5 minutes until tender but still firm.
